Frequently Asked
Questions ?
1. What courses do you offer?
We offer a variety of courses, including Graphic Design, Video Editing, Social Media Management, and more. Our courses cover both beginner and advanced levels, designed to take you from basics to pro-level skills.
2. Are the courses available both online and offline?
Yes! We offer both online and offline classes. Our offline classes are held at our Pune location, while online classes are conducted via Google Meet for easy access from anywhere.
3. Do I need prior experience to join the courses?
No prior experience is required. Our courses are structured to start from the basics and guide you through advanced techniques, ensuring that beginners can follow along while more experienced students can deepen their skills.
4. What kind of support will I get after completing a course?
After completing the course, you'll receive lifetime access to a WhatsApp group for ongoing queries and support. You'll also get design assets, font packs, and access to the best websites for design resources.
5. Do you provide certificates upon course completion?
Yes, we provide a professional certificate after you complete the course, showcasing your newly acquired skills to potential clients and employers.
6. How do I register for a course?
You can register for our courses through our website by selecting your preferred course and following the payment instructions. For any assistance, feel free to contact us directly via phone or Instagram DM.
